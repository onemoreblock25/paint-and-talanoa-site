Paint & Talanoa

A gentle, creative team-building experience grounded in art, storytelling, and connection — designed for teams who want to feel uplifted and edified.

What is Paint & Talanoa?

Paint & Talanoa weaves together guided painting and facilitated talanoa to create space for reflection, creativity, and meaningful connection. No art experience is required — just openness and presence.

This experience is especially suited for:
  • Workplace teams & organisations
  • Wellbeing days and retreats
  • Groups seeking deeper connection and renewal

The Experience

Short Session

A gentle introduction to painting and talanoa. Ideal for team meetings or wellbeing check-ins.

Standard Session

Guided creative practice, shared reflection, and group conversation designed to strengthen bonds.

Extended / Retreat Experience

A deeper, slower experience with tailored prompts, space for storytelling, and intentional facilitation.
